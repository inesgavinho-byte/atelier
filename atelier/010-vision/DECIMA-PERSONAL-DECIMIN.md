# DECIMA — Personal Decimin & Conversation Watch

## Core Idea

Every person can have a **Personal Decimin**.

For Inês, this is not a generic assistant.

It is a personal cognitive companion that helps organise work, thought, meetings, conversations, commitments and attention across every Space.

It supports the person.

It does not replace the person.

---

# Role

The Personal Decimin helps with:

* organising ideas;
* remembering context;
* preparing meetings;
* tracking unanswered requests;
* summarising conversations;
* structuring decisions;
* managing agenda;
* capturing loose thoughts;
* connecting personal work across Spaces.

It is the bridge between personal cognition and DECIMA Workspace.

---

# Core Principle

> **The Personal Decimin belongs to the user.
> The work produced inside a Space belongs to the Space.
> The knowledge extracted belongs to DECIMA.**

---

# Main Capabilities

## 1. Organise
Receives: notes, voice messages, screenshots, links, documents, messages, loose thoughts.
Transforms them into: Space updates, decisions, readings, captures, artifacts, reminders, next actions.

## 2. Think
Helps structure thinking. Turns scattered ideas into: theses, principles, decisions, project concepts, Papers, risks, opportunities, questions.

## 3. Prepare
Before a meeting, prepares: context, history, people involved, pending decisions, open risks, unanswered requests, recommended questions.

## 4. Accompany
During a meeting, if consent and permissions exist, it can: listen, transcribe, identify decisions, detect commitments, note unanswered questions, highlight relevant previous context.

## 5. Follow Up
After meetings or conversations, creates: summary, actions, responsible people, deadlines, decisions, pending replies, documents to update, calendar events.

---

# Conversation Watch

Conversation Watch is the Personal Decimin's ability to observe communication channels and prevent important things from disappearing.

Initial target: Telegram groups.
Future channels: WhatsApp, email, Teams, Slack, meetings, voice notes.

---

# Telegram Groups

The Personal Decimin can be added to Telegram groups as a bot.

Its role is not to dominate the group.
Its role is to observe and systematise.

---

# What It Extracts

From conversations, it identifies:
* requests;
* unanswered questions;
* commitments;
* promised files;
* links;
* deadlines;
* decisions;
* risks;
* opportunities;
* people responsible;
* follow-ups.

---

# Pending Requests

One of the most important functions:

> detect what Inês asked for and has not yet received.

Examples:
* "Inês asked João for the marcenaria quote three days ago. No answer yet."
* "Valentina said she would send the file yesterday. No attachment received."
* "A supplier promised revised pricing. Still pending."
* "A PR was requested but no update has appeared."

---

# Proactive Reminders

The Personal Decimin should not create noise.
It should only interrupt when there is meaningful pending work.

Example briefing:
```
Tens 4 pedidos sem resposta:

1. Restelo — orçamento Novibelo
2. DECIMA — PR por rever
3. Escola — confirmação pendente
4. PAPERS — fonte ainda não guardada
```

## Level 1 — Summarise
## Level 2 — Detect Pending Items

## Level 5 — Reply with Approval

Initial recommended level: Level 2 or 3.

---

# Privacy and Consent

Conversation Watch must be explicit.
The Decimin can only observe groups where it has been deliberately added.
It should always be obvious when the Decimin is present.

---

# Relationship with Spaces

A conversation may be personal or linked to a Space.

Telegram Group → Conversation Watch → Signal → GAVINHO Space → Project GA00462 → Pending supplier response

The Decimin routes relevant signals to the right Space.

---

# Personal Modes

* Chief Mode — maintains continuity across everything
* Thinking Mode — helps organise ideas and judgement
* Meeting Mode — prepares, accompanies and follows up meetings
* Focus Mode — protects attention, identifies what matters today
* Life Admin Mode — agenda, appointments, reminders, personal logistics
* Conversation Watch Mode — monitors channels, tracks pending commitments

---

# Foundational Rule

> **The Personal Decimin does not manage tasks.
> It manages continuity, clarity and follow-through.**

Its value is not answering questions.
Its value is ensuring that nothing important disappears.
