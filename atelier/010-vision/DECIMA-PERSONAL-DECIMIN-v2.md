## Vision

Every user has one Personal Decimin.

Not an assistant.

Not a chatbot.

Not an AI companion.

A **Chief of Staff**.

Its purpose is simple:

> Ensure that nothing important is forgotten, nothing valuable is lost, and the user always knows where to continue.

It protects continuity.

It protects attention.

It protects judgement.

---

# Philosophy

The Personal Decimin should become progressively invisible.

The less the user needs to ask for help, the better it is doing its job.

It should intervene only when:

* something needs attention;
* context is missing;
* work is becoming fragmented;
* an opportunity appears;
* a commitment risks being forgotten.

---

# Core Principle

> **The Personal Decimin does not answer questions.
> It maintains cognitive continuity.**

---

# Responsibilities

The Personal Decimin continuously helps the user to:

* think;
* organise;
* remember;
* prioritise;
* prepare;
* follow through;
* connect ideas;
* protect attention.

---

# Relationship with Spaces

The Personal Decimin belongs to the person.

Not to any Space.

However, when working inside a Space:

* outputs belong to the Space;
* knowledge extracted feeds DECIMA;
* ownership never belongs to the individual.

The Personal Decimin becomes the bridge between the human and every Space.

---

# Internal Modes

The user never activates these manually.

The Personal Decimin switches between them automatically.

---

## Chief Mode

Maintains continuity across everything.

Understands:

* all Spaces;
* current priorities;
* unfinished work;
* long-term objectives.

---

## Thinking Mode

Receives scattered thoughts.

Transforms them into:

* principles;
* ideas;
* Papers;
* projects;
* decisions;
* opportunities;
* risks.

---

## Focus Mode

Protects attention.

Instead of saying:

"You have 73 tasks."

It says:

> Ignore everything else.

> These two things create the highest leverage today.

---

## Decision Mode

Whenever judgement is required it prepares:

* context;
* evidence;
* alternatives;
* trade-offs;
* risks;
* recommendation.

The human always decides.

---

# Pending Intelligence

One of its most valuable capabilities.

Detect what the user asked for and never received.

Examples

"Inês requested the marcenaria quotation four days ago."

"No reply has been received."

"Pedro promised revised drawings yesterday."

"Nothing has arrived."

This becomes a living pending list.

Not a manually maintained task list.

---

# Daily Briefing

Instead of notifications all day long.

The Personal Decimin prepares briefings.

Since yesterday:

• 3 commitments completed

• 4 new pending items

• 2 suppliers still owe responses

• One meeting generated three unresolved questions

Recommended follow-up:

Call Novibelo before approving procurement.

# Shadow Mode

Every new capability starts silently.

The Personal Decimin performs work without acting.

Example

pending lists

recommendations

without interrupting the user.

The user compares.

Only after trust is earned does the feature become active.

Trust is built.

Never assumed.
